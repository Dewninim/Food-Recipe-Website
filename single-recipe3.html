French Bread Pizza

Introducing French Bread Pizza: a delightful fusion of French and Italian flavors! Crispy baguette slices serve as the perfect canvas for tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack, a tasty appetizer, or a fuss-free dinner option, each bite offers a symphony of textures and tastes. Elevate your pizza game with this simple yet irresistible dish that's sure to become a family favorite in no time!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : meat breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Preparation: Begin by preheating your oven to 400°F (200°C). While the oven heats up, slice a baguette lengthwise and place the halves on a baking sheet. Lightly toast the bread in the oven for a few minutes until it's slightly crispy.

step 2

Toppings: Once the bread is toasted, spread a layer of tomato sauce or marinara sauce evenly over each half of the baguette. Then, sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, olives, or mushrooms.

step 3

Bake and Enjoy: Place the topped baguette halves back in the oven and bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Once done, remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute or two, then slice into individual portions. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious French Bread Pizza!
