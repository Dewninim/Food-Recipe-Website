Banana Pancakes

Indulge in a delightful twist on breakfast classics with our tantalizing Banana Pancakes! Imagine fluffy golden pancakes infused with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds with every bite. Whether enjoyed drizzled with maple syrup, topped with a dollop of creamy yogurt, or sprinkled with crunchy nuts, these pancakes are sure to elevate your morning routine to a whole new level of deliciousness. prep time

30 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : meat breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Mash and Mix: Begin by mashing ripe bananas in a mixing bowl until smooth. Add in your favorite pancake mix or a homemade batter, along with a splash of milk, a dash of cinnamon, and a hint of vanilla extract for extra flavor. Stir until well combined.

step 2

Heat and Pour: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray. Once hot, pour a ladleful of the banana pancake batter onto the skillet, using the back of the ladle to spread it into a round shape. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles begin to form on the surface.

step 3

Flip and Serve: Carefully flip the pancake using a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side, or until golden brown and cooked through. Repeat with the remaining batter. Serve your delicious banana pancakes warm, topped with sliced bananas, a drizzle of honey or maple syrup, and a sprinkle of chopped nuts or chocolate chips for extra indulgence. Enjoy!

ingredients

Ripe bananas

Pancake mix or flour

Milk (or a dairy-free alternative)

Vanilla extract (optional)

Toppings of your choice, such as maple syrup, honey, sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or chocolate chips.
